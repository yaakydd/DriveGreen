üéØ SDG Alignment

üß† Data Science / AI Focus

üß† Data Science / AI Focus

Geospatial: Location data of the woman's home, clinic location, road network quality, and distance to the clinic.

Technique: Survival Analysis and Geospatial Machine Learning.
